Akan Names!

This is a web application that takes a user's birthday and calculates the day of the week they were born and then depending on their gender outputs their Akan Names.

The Following application should display names according to Day:

    Male

  1. Sunday: Kwasi
  2. Monday: Kwadwo
  3. Tuesday: Kwabena
  4. Wednesday: Yaw
  5. Thursday: Yaw
  6. Friday: kofi
  7. Saturday: Kwame

    Female

  1. Sunday: Akosua
  2. Monday: Adwoa
  3. Tuesday:Abenaa
  4. Wednesday: Akua
  5. Thursday: Yaa
  6. Friday: Afua
  7. Saturday: Ama

To use this application Enter your birthday then click on your gender and it display your day and birthday name;
